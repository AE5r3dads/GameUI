Stray INFO

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Stray is an upcoming adventure game developed by Blue-Twelve Studios and published by

Annapurna Interactive. The game was previously known as Project H-K. The game was launched on July 19, 2022 for

Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5. The story revolves around the story of a stray cat who falls into a world

inhabited by robots and ventures back to his family.
